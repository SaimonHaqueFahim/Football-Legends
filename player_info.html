Neymar

Neymar da Silva Santos Júnior, known as Neymar Júnior or mononymously as Neymar, is a Brazilian professional footballer who plays as a forward for Saudi Pro League club Al Hilal and the Brazil national team.

Born: February 5, 1992 (age 31 years), Mogi das Cruzes, State of São Paulo, Brazil

Current teams: Al Hilal SFC (#10 / Forward), Brazil national football team (#10 / Forward), Brazil

Height: 1.75 m

Salary: 40.8 million GBP (2022)

Parents: Neymar Santos Sr., Nadine Gonçalves

Teammates: Gabriel Barbosa, Thiago Silva, Marquinhos, Marcelo

Awards: Samba Gold, FIFA Puskás Award, FIFA FIFPro World XI
